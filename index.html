Your platform is not the problem. Your operating system is. Not software — the people, process, culture, and connective tissue between media, creative, production, and technology.

It was never content production. It is the quality of decisions at every stage — what to say, how to say it, where to place it, how to scale it. Every campaign is the product of that decision chain. The output is never better than the weakest link.
